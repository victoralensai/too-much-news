ABOUT PROJECT: TOO MUCH NEWS!!

I love my rss aggregator. At first, it was simple: a few blogs, a few newspapers. But soon, the addiction took over. I added everything. If a website had an RSS feed, I wanted it.

I ended up with so much content that I couldn't read 1% of it. Most of it wasn't even interesting. It took me weeks to prune the list back to sanity.

I'm now way happier with not as much content on my RSS feed. It's okay if I miss some things—I don't care. I'm glad with what I still learn every day.

As a joke, I wondered: "How much smarter could I become if I actually consumed it all?" This website is the answer to that joke. It is not a news tool; it is a visualization of the overwhelming chaos we produce every second.

My name is Victor Jean. I am a French student at ENSAI (National School for Statistics and Data Analysis in France), and a future data scientist/data engineer.

You can see who I am and what I'm up to at: vctor.me
