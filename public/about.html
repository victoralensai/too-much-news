ABOUT: TOO MUCH NEWS!!

I love my rss aggregator. A lot of websites I followed had great articles, but their RSS feeds were not curated. I kept adding more sources I liked, and the result was way too much content.

It became a bit addictive, but it did not feel dumb at the time. I genuinely thought it would work. Instead I ended up with so much content that I could not read 1% of it, and it took weeks to prune the list back to sanity.

I'm now way happier with not as much content on my RSS feed. It's okay if I miss some things—I don't care. I'm glad with what I still learn every day.

As a joke, I wondered: "How much smarter could I become if I actually consumed it all?" This website is an answer to that joke. Please do not use it to read news your brain might fry!

My name is Victor Jean. I am a French student at ENSAI (National School for Statistics and Data Analysis in France), and a future data scientist/data engineer.

You can see who I am and what I'm up to at: vctor.me
